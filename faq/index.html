frequently asked questions

1. I bought something. When do i get it?
  • Immediately after payment you will receive the key in your email address
2. I paid but did not receive anything, What should I do?
  • Check the email from which you paid for the purchase.
  • Probably your order status is pending and you will receive the product when the payment is completed.
  • Contact us and we will help you. To get your product as quickly as possible, please provide us with your email address you entered when purchasing
3. I created a support ticket but no one answers. What should I do?
  • You will definitely receive a response within a maximum of 24 hours.
  • If you have not received a reply within 24 hours you have the right to get a personal offer.
4. How do I leave a feedback on sellix.io?
  • To leave a feedback, Go to the Email containing your purchased product -> Leave a Feedback.
5. How do I update a feedback I previously left on sellix.io?
  • To update your feedback, Go to the Email containing your purchased product -> Leave a Feedback.
